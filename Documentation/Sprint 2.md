# User Stories

| Identifier |  User Story |
| ---------- | ------------- |
|US 1    | As an applicant, I should be able to create an account so that I can access funding opportunities. |
|US 2    | As an applicant, I should be able to submit funding applications for educational, business, or event initiatives. |
|US 3    | As an applicant, I should be able to upload attachments (such as documents or presentations) to support my funding application. |
|US 4    | As an applicant, I should be able to track the status of my funding applications and receive updates. |
|US 5    | As a fund manager, I should be able to advertise funding opportunities for educational, business, and event initiatives. |
|US 6    | As a fund manager, I should be able to review and approve/reject funding applications submitted by applicants. |
|US 7    | As a platform admin, I should be able to manage users, including reviewing and approving fund managers, blocking users, and managing permissions. |

# User Acceptance Tests

| Identifier |  User Story |
| ---------- | ------------- |
|US 1    | Given I am a user, when I navigate to the registration page, then the options to create an account with a 3rd party identity provider becomes visible. |
|US 2    | Given I am an applicant, when I submit a funding application, then I can select the type of funding opportunity (educational, business, or event). |
|US 3    | Given I am an applicant, when I submit a funding application, then I am able to upload attachments to support my application. |
|US 4    | Given I am an applicant, when I log in to my account, then I see the status of my funding applications and any updates. |
|US 5    | Given I am a fund manager, when I log in to my account, then I see the options to advertise funding opportunities for educational, business, and event initiatives. |
|US 6    | Given I am a fund manager, when I review funding applications, then I can approve or reject them. |
|US 7    | Given I am a platform admin, when I log in to my account, then I see options to manage users, including reviewing and approving fund managers, blocking users, and managing permissions. |
