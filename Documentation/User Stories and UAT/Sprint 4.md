# User Stories

| Identifier | User Story |
| ---------- | ------------- |
|US 1      | As a user, I should be able to register an account using a third-party identity provider, so that I can access the funding application platform securely. |
|US 2      | As a user, I should be able to log in using my third-party identity provider credentials, so that I can access my account. |
|US 3      | As a Fund Manager, I should be able to create and advertise funding opportunities, so that users can apply for funding. |
|US 4      | As a user, I should be able to apply for available funding opportunities, so that I can receive financial support for my needs. |
|US 5      | As a user, I should be able to upload and view attachments with my application, so that I can provide necessary documentation. |
|US 6      | As a user, I should be able to follow up on my applications and see status updates, so that I can stay informed about my application process. |
|US 7      | As a Fund Manager, I should be able to review and approve or reject applications, so that I can manage the funding opportunities effectively. |
|US 8      | As a Fund Manager, I should be able to view the total available budget and budget usage for my funding opportunities, so that I can effectively manage and allocate funds. |
|US 9      | As a Fund Manager, I should be able to allocate funds to approved applications, so that I can distribute the funds appropriately. |
|US 10      | As a Fund Manager, I should be able to generate reports on budget usage and allocation, so that I can track and analyze the budget effectively. |
|US 11      | As a Fund Manager, I want to be able to generate reports on application data, so that I can analyze and make informed decisions about funding opportunities. |
|US 12      | As a Fund Manager or Platform Admin, I want to be able to create custom reports based on specific criteria, so that I can analyze and report on specific aspects of the system. |
|US 13      | As a user, I should be able to receive notifications when the status of my application changes, so that I can stay informed about the progress of my application. |
|US 14      | As a Fund Manager, I should be able to receive notifications when new applications are submitted for my funding opportunities, so that I can review them in a timely manner. |

# User Acceptance Tests

| Identifier | User Story |
| ---------- | ------------- |
|US 1      | Given I am a user, when I register using a third-party identity provider, then my account is created and I can log in securely. |
|US 2      | Given I am a user, when I log in using my third-party identity provider credentials, then I am granted access to my account. |
|US 3      | Given I am a Fund Manager, when I create and publish a funding opportunity, then it is visible to all applicants on the platform. |
|US 4      | Given I am a user, when I apply for a funding opportunity, then my application is submitted and I receive a confirmation. |
|US 5      | Given I am a user, when I upload attachments with my application, then the attachments are saved and viewable within my application. |
|US 6      | Given I am a user, when I check the status of my application, then I can see updates and any changes made to the status. |
|US 7      | Given I am a Fund Manager, when I review applications, then I can approve or reject them and the applicant is notified of my decision. |
|US 8      | Given I am a Fund Manager, when I view the budget overview page, then I can see the total available budget and the budget usage over time, including allocated and remaining funds. |
|US 9      | Given I am a Fund Manager, when I allocate funds to approved applications, then the allocated funds are deducted from the total available budget, and the budget usage is updated accurately. |
|US 10      | Given I am a Fund Manager, when I generate budget reports, then I can see relevant information such as total budget, allocated funds, remaining funds, and fund allocation details, and export the reports in CSV or PDF format. |
|US 11      | Given I am a Fund Manager, when I generate application data reports, then I can see relevant information such as the number of applications received, approved, rejected, and pending review, and filter and sort the reports based on various criteria, and export the reports in CSV or PDF format. |
|US 12      | Given I am a Fund Manager or Platform Admin, when I create custom reports, then I can specify the desired data fields and filtering criteria, and generate and export the custom reports in CSV or PDF format. |
|US 13      | Given I am a user, when my application status changes (e.g., submitted, under review, approved, rejected), then I receive a notification with relevant information about the status change through email or in-app notifications. |
|US 14      | Given I am a Fund Manager, when new applications are submitted for my funding opportunities, then I receive notifications with relevant information about the submitted application through email or in-app notifications. |
