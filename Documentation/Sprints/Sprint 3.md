# User Stories

| Identifier |  User Story |
| ---------- | ------------- |
|US 1    | As a user, I should be able to create an account so that I can access funding opportunities. |
|US 2    | As a user, I should be able to submit funding applications for educational, business, or event initiatives. |
|US 3    | As a user, I should be able to upload attachments (such as documents or presentations) to support my funding application. |
|US 4    | As a user, I should be able to track the status of my funding applications and receive updates. |
|US 5    | As a fund manager, I should be able to advertise funding opportunities for educational, business, and event initiatives. |
|US 6    | As a fund manager, I should be able to review and approve/reject funding applications submitted by users. |
|US 7    | As a platform admin, I should be able to manage users, including reviewing and approving fund managers, blocking users, and managing permissions. |
|US 8    | As a user, I should be able to filter funding opportunities based on criteria such as funding type, deadline, and eligibility criteria. |
|US 9    | As a user, I should receive notifications or emails regarding the status of my funding application, such as when it has been reviewed or approved/rejected. |
|US 10   | As a fund manager, I should be able to set criteria and requirements for each funding opportunity, such as minimum results attained for educational scholarships or business plan requirements for business funding. |
|US 11   | As a platform admin, I should be able to generate and export reports on funding applications, including details such as applicant demographics, funding amounts requested, and funding types. |
|US 12   | As a user, I should be able to save draft versions of my funding applications and return to complete them later. |
|US 13   | As a fund manager, I should be able to allocate funds to specific funding opportunities and track the usage of allocated funds. |
|US 14   | As a platform admin, I should be able to customize the application's branding and appearance. |

# User Acceptance Tests

| Identifier |  User Story |
| ---------- | ------------- |
|US 1    | Given I am a user, when I navigate to the registration page, then the options to create an account with a 3rd party identity provider becomes visible. |
|US 2    | Given I am a user, when I submit a funding application, then I can select the type of funding opportunity (educational, business, or event). |
|US 3    | Given I am a user, when I submit a funding application, then I am able to upload attachments to support my application. |
|US 4    | Given I am a user, when I log in to my account, then I see the status of my funding applications and any updates. |
|US 5    | Given I am a fund manager, when I log in to my account, then I see the options to advertise funding opportunities for educational, business, and event initiatives. |
|US 6    | Given I am a fund manager, when I review funding applications, then I can approve or reject them. |
|US 7    | Given I am a platform admin, when I log in to my account, then I see options to manage users, including reviewing and approving fund managers, blocking users, and managing permissions. |
|US 8    | Given I am a user, when I navigate to the funding opportunities page, then I can filter options such as funding type, deadline, and eligibility criteria. |
|US 9    | Given I am a user, when my funding application status changes (e.g., reviewed, approved, rejected), then I receive an email notification with the update. |
|US 10   | Given I am a fund manager, when I create a funding opportunity, then I am able to specify criteria and requirements, such as minimum results attained or business plan submission. |
|US 11   | Given I am a platform admin, when I access the reporting dashboard, then I can generate reports on funding applications, including applicant demographics and funding details. |
|US 12   | Given I am a user, when I start filling out a funding application, then I have the option to save it as a draft and continue editing it later. |
|US 13   | Given I am a fund manager, when I allocate funds to a funding opportunity, then I am able to track the usage of allocated funds and see how much has been spent. |
|US 14   | Given I am a platform admin, when I access the branding settings, then I can customize  the application's appearance. |
